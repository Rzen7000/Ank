Caw, Caw: Send a Message

I don’t do social media. I have enough existential dread without watching people argue about whether you should peel a banana from the stem or the bottom.
Bennu agrees; he’s a bird of few taps.

If you need to reach me; to ask a question, share an exotic fruit recommendation, or tell me my dark chocolate take was wrong, email is the only way.

Email: 3ankaa@proton.me

Response time: Don’t expect an immediate response. If you’re lucky, within a week.
If I’m deep in a rabbit hole or traveling for mangoes, maybe a month. I’ll get to you. Probably.

Sarcastic warning (because you’ve made it this far):
- No, I will not help you name your houseplant.
- No, I will not translate Bennu’s taps for you. He’s cryptic on purpose.
- No, I will not tell you where the Imaginarium is. You have to get lost first.
- No, I will not fix your snack machine. Call Adam and Hani, they will build you a better one.
- No, I will not explain why Jimba’s backpack is a portal to another dimension (it’s snacks).
- Yes, you can send me dark chocolate. I will not share my address, but the thought counts.
- Yes, you can name a character after yourself in a future Quest Pack. For a small donation to my mango fund.
(Kidding. Or am I? Honestly, I’d probably do it for free, I’m easy.)

If you made it through all that and still want to email me, you’re my kind of weirdo. Hit me up.
Bennu might even tap a greeting, or he might ignore you. He’s a bird of mystery.
