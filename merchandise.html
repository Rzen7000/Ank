Now let’s talk about hoodies.

I love them. You love them. The world needs more hoodies with weird prints;
like a hoopoe wearing a graduation cap, or a diagram of a snack‑powered machine.

So I’m enthusiastically selling hoodies. Well, I will be. Eventually.

Sometimes I’d rather give them away, because capitalism is exhausting,
and I just want to buy dark chocolate and exotic fruits.
But chocolate money doesn’t grow on trees
(except cocoa trees, but that’s farming, and I respect farmers).
