🐦 Who's Who in the Imaginarium

Meet the weirdos, the snack‑lovers, the hoopoe, and the ones who accidentally save the world.

🐦 Bennu (the Hoopoe)

Tap language: 1 tap = yes, 2 taps = no, 3 taps = snack time, 4 taps = you're being ridiculous.

Bennu is ancient, sarcastic, and may or may not control the Imaginarium. He appears when least expected, taps his beak, and leaves more questions than answers. Favourite food: sunflower seeds (but he'll never admit it).

🍫 Jimba

Snack‑obsessed. Loyal. Has a backpack that sometimes becomes a portal to another dimension (it's the snacks). Jimba doesn't like reading long paragraphs, but he'll read an entire adventure if there's a chocolate reward at the end.

📚 Ali

The quiet one. The thinker. Ali takes notes when others are arguing. He's the one who asks, "But why do we have homework if we already learned it?" and then actually listens to the answer. Recently became the "Accidental Messenger".

🎒 The Storykeeper (You know who)

Former teacher, current dreamer. Addicted to dark chocolate and exotic fruits. Writes things down so you don't have to. Also the only human Bennu tolerates (mostly).

🥭 The Mango Committee

A running joke that appears in footnotes. No one knows who they are, but they approve all exotic fruit‑related decisions.

More characters appear as the Quest Packs unfold. Bennu refuses to give spoilers.
