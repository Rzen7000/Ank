About the person behind the PDFs

Former teacher. Current dreamer. Taurus (yes, stubborn). Truth-seeker who keeps finding more questions. Creative when the dark chocolate levels are optimal. Loves reading, writing, and traveling to places where exotic fruits grow.

This digital library exists because sharing is caring, and caring is free. No ads, no tracking, no algorithms trying to sell you things. Just a weird little corner of the internet for fellow weirdos who like serious stuff wrapped in funny words.

Warning: I am addicted to dark chocolate (80% cocoa minimum) and exotic fruits from around the world. If you ever send me a mango I haven't tried, you will become my favorite person.

Questions? Want to say hi? Check the Contact page. But don't expect instant gratification – I might be eating chocolate or staring at a fruit.
