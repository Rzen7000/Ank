📖 The Storykeeper

Former teacher. Current dreamer. Stubborn? Maybe a little. Truth‑seeker who keeps finding more questions instead of answers.
Creative when the dark chocolate levels are just right (80% cocoa minimum; don't @ me).

I love reading in weird places, writing stories that argue with themselves, and traveling to places where exotic fruits grow, preferably where fruit tastes like candy.
I also love Bennu; a hoopoe who may or may not be magic.
He lives in my imagination, and sometimes in my classroom, pecking at lesson plans.

This digital library exists because sharing is caring, and caring is free.
No ads, no tracking, no creepy algorithms trying to sell you things.
Just a weird little corner of the internet for fellow weirdos who like serious stuff wrapped in funny words.

Warning: I am addicted to dark chocolate and exotic fruits from faraway places.
If you ever send me a unique mango or a fruit I haven't tried, you will become my favourite person.
But please don't actually send fruit; customs is a nightmare. Send a photo instead.

Questions? Want to say hi? Check Caw, Caw: Send a Message. But don't expect an instant reply;
I might be eating chocolate, staring at fruit, or arguing with a fictional hoopoe about the best way to enter a magical Imaginarium.

The Quiet Philosophy
(It's not loud. But it's important.)
I don't publish the full blueprint online, that's for the printed books.
But here's the heart of it:
#Learn by living, not by memorising.
#Laugh hard, think harder.
#Discomfort is not danger, it's growth.
#Egypt isn't a backdrop; it's a root.
#Small actions matter. A tap. A seed. A shared snack.

If that speaks to you, the books will speak louder.
