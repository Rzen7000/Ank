🍎 For Educators: Bring the Imaginarium to Your Classroom

You teach. You care. You also want to keep your sanity.
These Quest Packs are designed to make serious topics feel like adventures, without the standard "worksheet fatigue".

📖 How to use a Quest Pack

Each Quest Pack includes:
- A complete story (no abridgement)
- "Imaginarium Kit": hands‑on activities using simple materials
- "Bennu's Field Log": portfolio prompts (draw, write, debate)
- Cross‑curricular links (history, science, ethics, art)
- "Glorious Failures" section – because trying and falling is learning

🧠 Sample Discussion Questions

  • Why does Jimba's backpack become a portal when snacks are inside? (Metaphor for hidden potential?)
  • In "Silence of the Hive", who was right: The Farmer or the teacher? Can both be right?
  • Bennu never gives direct answers. Is that teaching or just annoying?

🎓 Curriculum Alignment (General)

Quest Packs touch on:
- Social‑emotional learning (empathy, fairness)
- Critical thinking (multiple perspectives)
- Ethical reasoning (Zakat, freedom of speech, animal testing)
- Creative writing and visual storytelling
